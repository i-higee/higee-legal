Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect a Bluetooth device?
Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your device. Open the APP and it will automatically scan for nearby Bluetooth devices. Tap the desired device to connect. If no devices are found, please ensure the device is in discoverable mode.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored only locally on your device. The APP does not upload any data to the cloud, nor does it collect your personal private information.
Does the APP require an internet connection?
The APP is designed primarily for local offline use. All features work normally without an internet connection. It only connects briefly to the internet when you manually check for version updates.
How do I provide feedback or suggestions?
You can contact us via email at huac@ihigee.com. We will respond to your feedback promptly.
